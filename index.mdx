Actioneer is enterprise AI that answers any data question and acts on it. Connect your data once — your warehouse, your event streams, your attribution data — and query all of it in natural language, governed and read-only, with an audit trail on every action.

These docs cover the two ways you bring data into Actioneer: **connecting a data source** Actioneer reads in place, and **streaming events** to the ingestion API in real time. Pick the path that matches your stack below.

## Choose your path

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Connect a data source" icon="database" href="/integrations/data-warehouses">
    Give Actioneer read-only access to a warehouse, lake, or BI source — Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift, Databricks, and more. Actioneer queries it in place; no data migration.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stream events in real time" icon="send" href="/ingestion">
    Push analytics events, identity calls, and profile updates straight from your app or event SDK over one JSON-over-HTTPS API — no warehouse round-trip.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Why Actioneer

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Governance-first" icon="shield-check">
    Your data team owns the definitions. Every connection is least-privilege and read-only, and every action Actioneer takes is auditable.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Operational continuity" icon="workflow">
    Actioneer fits into your existing stack rather than replacing it — it reads your sources where they already live and leaves your pipelines untouched.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Proven accuracy" icon="award">
    Actioneer leads public text-to-SQL benchmarks, including DABstep, DA-Bench, and KRAMAbench — accuracy you can put in front of a board.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
