Stuck on a step, hitting an error, or weighing an architecture decision? Here's how to get help, starting with the fastest options.

## Get help

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Search the docs" icon="search">
    Press <kbd>Cmd</kbd>/<kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>K</kbd> to find any page instantly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Ask AI" icon="sparkles">
    Use **Ask AI** in these docs for an answer synthesized from the documentation, with links to the source.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email the team" icon="mail" href="mailto:connect@actioneer.com">
    Reach us at [connect@actioneer.com](mailto:connect@actioneer.com) and a member of the team will follow up.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
  Working on an integration? Including the source or endpoint, the step you're on, and any error message helps us resolve it faster.
</Info>

## Report a security issue

If you believe you've found a vulnerability, please disclose it responsibly:

- Email [connect@actioneer.com](mailto:connect@actioneer.com) with **"Security"** in the subject line.
- Include steps to reproduce and the potential impact.
- Please give us a reasonable window to investigate and remediate before any public disclosure.

<Note>
  Actioneer is SOC 2 Type II certified, ISO 27001 certified (audited by Schellman), and GDPR compliant. Credentials are encrypted at rest with AES-256 and in transit with TLS 1.3. See [Access & networking](/operations/access-and-networking).
</Note>
