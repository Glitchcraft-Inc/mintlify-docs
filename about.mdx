# What is Actioneer

Actioneer is an AI coworker for growth and business teams. It sits on top of your existing data stack — warehouses, BI tools, CDPs, and CLMs — and lets anyone on the team go from question to insight to action, all in natural language, without switching tools.

Most B2C companies already have dashboards, analytics platforms, and messaging tools. The problem is not a lack of data. It is that data lives in one place, decisions happen in another, and action happens in a third. By the time someone on the growth team notices a drop in conversion, pulls a report, shares it on Slack, gets alignment, and actually runs a campaign to fix it, the window has passed.

Actioneer collapses that loop.

---

## Who is Actioneer for

Actioneer is built for operators — growth leads, product managers, marketing teams, and business analysts at B2C and fintech companies who need answers from data every day but do not want to wait on analyst queues or learn SQL.

| Role | How they use Actioneer |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Growth lead** | Ask ad-hoc questions in plain English, get granular breakdowns in seconds |
| **Product manager** | Monitor funnel health, spot anomalies early, act on them immediately |
| **Marketing team** | Build hyper-targeted segments and push campaigns from the same window |
| **Business analyst** | Offload tier-one requests, focus on deeper modelling and experimentation |

## Where Actioneer sits in the stack

Actioneer is not a replacement for your data warehouse, BI tool, or CDP. It is an intelligence layer that sits across all of them.

Your data warehouse stores the raw data. Your BI tool visualises predefined dashboards. Your CDP stitches user profiles. Actioneer connects to all three and adds what none of them offer on their own: natural-language querying with business context, persistent memory of your KPIs and playbooks, and the ability to act on insights without leaving the platform.

<Info>
  Actioneer connects to your existing infrastructure. It does not require data migration, pipeline rebuilds, or schema changes.
</Info>

## Why Actioneer

Three layers, stacked on top of your existing tools:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Query layer">
    A natural-language interface to your data warehouse. Ask a question in plain English, get an answer grounded in your schema and metric definitions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Memory layer">
    Actioneer remembers your business context — KPI definitions, past campaigns, playbooks, and team preferences. Every conversation builds on the last.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Action layer">
    When you see an insight, act on it from the same window. Build a segment, launch a campaign, push to your CLM, or set up an alert — without switching tools.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The combination is the point. A BI tool gives you the query layer. A CDP gives you fragments of the action layer. Actioneer ties all three together with compounding business context in between.

## Security and compliance

All credentials and data are encrypted with AES-256 at rest and transmitted over TLS 1.3. Actioneer never stores raw customer data in plain text.

| Certification | Status |
| :--- | :--- |
| **SOC 2 Type II** | Certified |
| **ISO 27001** | Certified (audited by Schellman) |
| **GDPR** | Compliant |

Role-based access control, full audit logs, and explainable AI are built in. Every query is traceable — the underlying SQL is always visible, never a black box.
