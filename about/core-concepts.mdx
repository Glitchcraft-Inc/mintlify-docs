## How they connect

The concepts above are not standalone features. They form a loop:

1. **Ask** — Start with a question in Chat. Get an answer grounded in your Metrics and Knowledge.
2. **Analyse** — Explore the answer through Funnels, Retention cohorts, or Segments.
3. **Persist** — Save findings to a Board or codify the methodology as a Playbook.
4. **Act** — Push a Segment to a Campaign, set up an alert, or schedule a Playbook to run automatically.

Every feature feeds back into the Knowledge layer. The more Actioneer is used, the better it understands your business.
