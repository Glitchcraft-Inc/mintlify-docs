# Getting Started

This guide walks through setting up Actioneer — from account creation to your first connected data source. The process takes approximately 15 minutes.

---

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- Access credentials for at least one data warehouse (Snowflake, BigQuery, Databricks, Redshift, or S3) or BI tool (Amplitude, Mixpanel, or Power BI).
- Administrative or project-level permissions on the source platform to create service accounts or API keys.

<Info>
  If you are unsure which credentials you need, see the specific integration guide for your platform under [Data Warehouses](/integrations/data-warehouses) or [BI Tools](/integrations/bi-tools).
</Info>

---

## Step-by-step setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Visit [actioneer.com](https://www.actioneer.com) and request access. Your team will receive an onboarding invitation with login credentials and workspace setup instructions.

    Each workspace is scoped to your organisation. Multiple team members can be added to the same workspace.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect a data source">
    Once logged in, navigate to **Data** in the left sidebar. Select your data warehouse or BI tool from the list of available integrations.

    Each integration requires a set of credentials specific to the platform — a service account JSON key for BigQuery, an access key pair for S3, or an API token for Amplitude, for example.

    Follow the integration guide for your platform:

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Data Warehouses" icon="database" href="/integrations/data-warehouses">
        BigQuery, Databricks, Snowflake, S3, Redshift
      </Card>
      <Card title="BI Tools" icon="chart-bar" href="/integrations/bi-tools">
        Amplitude, Mixpanel, Power BI
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Schema discovery">
    After connecting, Actioneer automatically discovers your schema — tables, columns, relationships, and event taxonomy. This process runs in the background and typically completes within a few minutes.

    Once discovery is complete, Actioneer is ready to receive questions about your data.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Onboarding call">
    Actioneer's team schedules a discovery call to map your existing metric definitions, KPI targets, and playbooks. This context is loaded into the Knowledge layer so that answers are grounded in your business logic from day one.

    The onboarding call is typically 30–60 minutes and covers:
    - Key metric definitions (what "conversion," "retention," and your north-star metrics mean at your company).
    - Existing dashboards and reports that Actioneer should be aware of.
    - Priority use cases for your team.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start asking questions">
    Open Chat and ask your first question. Actioneer uses your schema, metric definitions, and business context to generate an accurate, traceable answer.

    Try starting with something your team asks frequently — a weekly KPI check, a funnel breakdown, or a segment count. The answer will include the SQL used, so you can validate it against your existing reports.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## What happens next

Once your first data source is connected and the onboarding call is complete, your team can:

- **Ask ad-hoc questions** in Chat and get answers in seconds.
- **Build Boards** to persist findings and monitor KPIs.
- **Create Segments** and push them to your CLM for campaigns.
- **Save Playbooks** from validated analyses and schedule them to run automatically.

The platform compounds over time. Every question, correction, and validated playbook adds to the Knowledge layer, making subsequent answers faster and more precise.

## Common questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does setup take?">
    Connecting a data source takes 10–15 minutes. Schema discovery runs automatically after connection. The onboarding call is typically scheduled within the first week and takes 30–60 minutes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I connect multiple data sources?">
    Yes. Actioneer supports multiple warehouse and BI tool connections within a single workspace. Each connection is managed independently.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What permissions does Actioneer need?">
    Actioneer operates as a read-only integration by default. It queries your data but does not create, modify, or delete anything in your warehouse or connected tools. The specific permissions vary by platform — see the individual integration guides for details.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is my data secure?">
    All credentials are encrypted with AES-256 at rest and transmitted over TLS 1.3. Actioneer is SOC 2 Type II certified, ISO 27001 certified, and GDPR compliant. Role-based access control and full audit logs are built in.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
