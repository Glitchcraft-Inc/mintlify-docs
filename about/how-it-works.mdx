# How It Works

Actioneer sits as a single intelligence and action layer across your entire data estate. It does not replace any tool in your stack. It connects to what you already have and adds capabilities no single tool provides: governed context, agent orchestration, automated playbooks, and a direct path from insight to action.

The customer interacts through three operational surfaces — **Web**, **Slack**, and **CLI** (via Claude). The platform handles everything else.

## The Seven Layers

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Layer 1 — Enterprise Data Connectors" icon="plug" defaultOpen>
    Connects to 700+ data sources out of the box. Read-only by default. No data migration, no pipeline rebuilds.

    - **Breadth** — BI tools, analytics platforms, CRM, ad platforms, databases, Slack, payment systems, product databases
    - **Read-only** — Data stays in place — Actioneer reads it where it lives
    - **Custom API** — Available for sources not natively supported
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Layer 2 — Context Engine" icon="brain">
    The semantic layer that gives every AI model a governed, business-specific understanding of the organisation.

    - **Business mapping** — Automatically maps the app's signals and causal relationships
    - **Metric definitions** — Codified once, shared across every capability
    - **North-star goals** — Understands which metrics matter and what moves them
    - **Ownership graph** — Maps which team or person owns each data asset
    - **Lineage** — Definitions and their provenance tracked across the full data estate

    <Info>
      This is what stops the AI from hallucinating plausibly when it doesn't actually know what "revenue" or "active user" or "Tier-1 customer" means inside your organisation. The model reasons over a structured, governed representation of the business — not raw data, not its own training assumptions.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Layer 3 — Agent Orchestration Runtime" icon="cpu">
    The execution layer that runs single and multi-agent workflows at enterprise scale.

    - **Multi-agent** — A single question can trigger agents across every corner of the data estate simultaneously
    - **Model abstraction** — Models can be swapped or upgraded without breaking workflows
    - **Tool integration** — Agents connect to operational systems, not just data stores
    - **Policy enforcement** — Enforced at every step of execution
    - **Detect → Reason → Act → Learn** — Continuous loop that improves with every interaction
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Layer 4 — Playbook Studio" icon="book-open">
    Where multi-step capabilities are defined, tested, deployed, and refined.

    - **Playbooks** — UA reviews, churn scans, lifecycle analyses — built once, run automatically
    - **Scheduled monitoring** — Playbooks that run on schedule and deliver graded reports (A–E) per title, region, or team
    - **Proactive alerts** — When CAC spikes or retention drops, Actioneer sends a Slack alert with root cause attached
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Layer 5 — Insight-to-Action Layer" icon="zap">
    Closes the loop between query and execution.

    - **Segment push** — One-click push to CleverTap, WebEngage, MoEngage, Braze
    - **Cohort to campaign** — Identify high-value users and reach them before the moment passes
    - **Anomaly to fix** — Spot it, plan it, push it — without leaving the tab
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Layer 6 — Continuous Observability" icon="eye">
    Trace-level visibility into every agent decision, every playbook run, every model call.

    - **SQL transparency** — All answers include the SQL query used — auditable and traceable
    - **Performance analytics** — Drift detection, execution metrics, and quality monitoring
    - **Confidence flagging** — Low-confidence queries are flagged — no hallucinations permitted
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Layer 7 — Enterprise Governance" icon="shield-check">
    - **RBAC** — Who builds what, who runs what, who sees what
    - **Audit logs** — Full trail for all data access and agent actions
    - **Explainable AI** — No black box answers — reasoning and SQL visible on every response
    - **Private cloud** — Custom deployment available for institutions that require it
    - **Data residency** — US, EU, APAC options
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## What Happens When You Ask a Question

<Steps>
  <Step title="Question received">
    You ask a question in plain English. Actioneer parses the intent, data scope, and relevant signals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Context loaded">
    The Context Engine loads your metric definitions, north-star goals, ownership graph, and any relevant playbooks. The query is grounded in your business logic — not the model's assumptions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Agents dispatched">
    The Agent Orchestration Runtime routes the question to one or more agents. For complex questions, multiple agents span different corners of the data estate simultaneously.
  </Step>
  <Step title="SQL generated and executed">
    Queries are generated, validated against governed definitions, and executed against your connected sources. Every query is traceable.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Answer delivered">
    Results are returned as a chart, table, or metric — with the SQL and reasoning visible alongside the answer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Action available">
    From the same result, push a segment, trigger a campaign, set an alert, pin to a Board, or save as a Playbook.
  </Step>
</Steps>
